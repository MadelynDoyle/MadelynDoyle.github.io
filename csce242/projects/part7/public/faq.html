What do we do here?

At Palmetto Fresh Meats we process and pack beef and pork. Special requests can be made and large orders can be placed!

Where do we get our livestock?

Our cattle is raised on HD3 Cattle farm right herein Aynor, SC. Our cattle are on a feeding program that is ground fresh on the farm. We get our hogs from local farmers in the area.

Do we do custom processing?

We do custom processing for the public, where customers can get speciality cuts, bulk orders, as well as bring in their own livestock to be processed.

Do we process deer?

Currently we are not processing any game only cattle and hogs.
